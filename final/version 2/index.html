How to Play

This game is based on VALORANT

Roll:

  • Your damage is randomized based on the sum of two dice
  • There is a 10% chance you might hit a headshot where it doubles the damage
  • If one of the dice rolls a "1," your turn ends.
  • If both dice rolls a "1," your enemy regains full health

Pass:
When passing, you will earn a light shield (+25 health) and your turn will end.

First person to get the enemy's health down to zero wins.
